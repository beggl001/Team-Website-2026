Your Vibes, Managed

The Modern Jukebox for Local Spaces

The Problem

In the modern hospitality industry, atmosphere is everything. However, small cafes and local restaurants face a major hurdle: managing their background music. Currently, business owners are stuck between a few options, but all have major drawbacks. Some are bulky and costly to maintain, while others offer no customization of songs. For staff, manual song requests are a tedious distraction during busy rushes. For owners, the lack of a controlled, affordable, and legal way to let customers interact with the music means missing out on both customer engagement and a secondary revenue stream.

Problem Characteristics

  • Users/Customers - Do not have access or ability to play their favorite songs in local venues like gyms and cafes
  • Business Owners - Rely on simple playlists with no way to allow customer song requests
  • Stakeholders - Artists, record labels, and music platforms have limited reach to small businesses

Our Solution

VibeQueue is a web-based ecosystem that automates music selection by providing a digital interface for customers to browse and request songs via QR codes. It eliminates manual work and replaces expensive proprietary hardware with a software-only solution.

  • Seamless API Integration - Leverages Spotify API and SoundMachine for fully licensed, legal music
  • Low-Cost & Interactive - Affordable "Vibe Management" tool for small businesses
  • QR Code Access - Customers scan to browse and request songs
  • Business Control Center - Dashboard for owners to restrict genres and block explicit content

Customers, End-Users & Stakeholders

End Users: Customers at local businesses (coffee shops, restaurants, gyms) who want to influence the music

Customers (Businesses): Small local business owners who want an affordable music solution

Stakeholders: Artists, record labels, and music platforms (Spotify, SoundMachine)
